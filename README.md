# Advent of Code 2025

The first time I saw [Advent of Code](https://adventofcode.com/2025) was way back in... two years ago. Back then, I was barely starting to look at C. I gave it a quick go, but soon realized I was way over my head.
Two years later, I have barely started looking at C, and I am still way over my head.

## Before I start

### NOPY - Do not copy-paste code

Never copy-paste. Even for our own code. If you got there once, you can get there twice.

### NOOGLE - Do not google

You don't Google solutions, unless you have been stuck for AT LEAST 3 days.
If you have to Google, don't forget the NOPY rule. Study the solution, close the browser, and battle away.

### NOORAL - Do not use neural networks

Just don't.

## Running the project

### Prerequisites

- clang
- clangd
- bear

### Execution

There's a `build.sh` script in the root folder. You simply need to run it.

Inside it, there is a `day` variable. Change it to match whichever day you are trying to run.
